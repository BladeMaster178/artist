Siarhei Trukhan Biography

Sergei Trukhan was born in Minsk, Russia in 1970 and demonstrated his artistic gift at a very young age. He constantly held pencils, paints or camera in hand to capture all creative expression. When he enrolled in the Army, he discovered other artists with whom he would later serve as apprentice. They shared his intense passion for painting. And, because he is responsible for developing his own artistic skills, Sergei considers himself to be self-taught. When beauty and light are found in the details of nature, Sergei has the ability to capture the essence of light with exquisite wonder. The harmony he achieves in his paintings brings life to any lyrical, traditional or contemporary landscape. With a profound vision and love for nature, he paints what we perceive--and so much more. Scholars have come to recognize this style as "Hypo Realism", where detail and technical skills come together and are brilliantly demonstrated. Sergei strongly emphasizes the penetration of light into darkness as a "luminescent" style. Thus, his hope is that appreciation for his art will create gratitude for nature and our surroundings, and will spawn an awe-inspiring awakening for simple yet complex artistic expression. His creative artistry reveals the artist’s unique outlook: sunlit and full of life with a spiritual concordance with Nature and the Land--all of which captures light in unusual ways. Sergei contemplates and then delivers the multi-faceted world in which he feels interconnected. Sergei currently resides in Belarus, Russia with his wife and two sons. His popularity as an artist has quickly reached international acclaim as his technique, talent and eye for the unique and unusual scene has achieved the rank of Master Artist.
